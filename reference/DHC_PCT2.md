# Glossary for table PCT2 in the Demographic and Housing Characteristics survey.

Glossary for table PCT2 in the Demographic and Housing Characteristics
survey.

## Usage

``` r
DHC_PCT2
```

## Format

a data frame with seven columns

- Index:

  `<int>` the row number of the variable

- Level of Aggregation:

  `<int>` the level of aggregation of the variable

- One Person:

  `<lgl>` single-person or multi-person household

- Children:

  `<lgl>` are children present in the household

- Family:

  `<lgl>` are the dwellers a family

- Male Householder:

  `<lgl>` is there a male head of household

- Female Householder:

  `<lgl>` is there a female head of household
