## Demographic and Housing Characteristics

Groups from the Demographic and Housing Characteristics product

- [`DHC_PCT2`](https://higherx4racine.github.io/hercdectables/reference/DHC_PCT2.md)

  Characteristics survey.
- [`DHC_VARIABLES`](https://higherx4racine.github.io/hercdectables/reference/DHC_VARIABLES.md)

## Tenure

Tables for looking at home ownership and renting

- [`GLOSSARY_OF_TENURE_BY_ETHNICITY_AND_RACE`](https://higherx4racine.github.io/hercdectables/reference/GLOSSARY_OF_TENURE_BY_ETHNICITY_AND_RACE.md)

## Helpers

Functions for creating/working with glossaries about the Decennial
Census.

- [`fetch_variable_details()`](https://higherx4racine.github.io/hercdectables/reference/fetch_variable_details.md)
  : Download and parse the JSON representation of a decennial table's
  variables
- [`hoist_group_details()`](https://higherx4racine.github.io/hercdectables/reference/hoist_group_details.md)
  : Unpack the ragged list of details about a group's variables
- [`syabsbre()`](https://higherx4racine.github.io/hercdectables/reference/syabsbre.md)
  : Fetch population data broken down by single-year age, sex, and
  race/ethnicity
